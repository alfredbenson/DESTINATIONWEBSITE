What is the Most Beautiful Places in Philippines?

#1 Top Best

Banawe Rice Terraces

Banaue Rice Terraces are an extensive network of ancient rice fields carved into the mountains of Ifugao, in the northern Philippines. These terraces are often called the "Eighth Wonder of the World."

#2 Top Best Places

Cardillera Mountain

Cordillera Mountain is a prominent and extensive mountain range in the northern Philippines. It stretches across several provinces in the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR). This range is known for its rugged terrain, diverse ecosystems, and rich cultural heritage.

Visit Now >

#3 Top Best Places

Baguio City

Baguio is a highly urbanized city located in the northern part of the Philippines, within the province of Benguet in the Cordillera Administrative Region. It is often referred to as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" because of its cool climate and high altitude, making it a popular retreat for both locals and tourists seeking respite from the heat in the lowlands.

About

The Philippines is an archipelagic country in Southeast Asia, known for its rich history, diverse culture, stunning landscapes, and warm hospitality. It is composed of over 7,600 islands, making it one of the largest archipelagos in the world. The country's strategic location, history of colonization, and cultural exchanges have made it a melting pot of influences from Asia, Europe, and the Americas.
